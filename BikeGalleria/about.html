BikeGalleria is a branch of Bikers Group - a two decade old company in London,UK that deals in bikes. BikeGalleria has outlets both in New Delhi (2009) and now in Bangalore and Hyderabad (2012).

Conveniently located in the heart of Bangalore, at Infantry Road and Koramangala,BikeGalleria is one of the few showrooms that specialize in bikes.
